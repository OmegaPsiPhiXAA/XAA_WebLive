On December 6, 1980 in Spokane Washington six Lampados, Leon Skip Rowland, Bill Fountain, Jack Hall, John Steel, Billy V Morris Sr and Fred Douglas Mitchell guided by their DP Sam Baynes stepped into the light of Omega and became the Six Men of Light of Zeta Upsilon and later charter members of Chi Alpha Alpha. Sam Baynes, Rudy Boone Jr., Thomas Franklin, and Richard Williams made it possible for these six men to enter into the light of Omega and give birth to a chapter which has now become a vital component to many communities in the area. Chi Alpha Alpha now encompesses Washington State University, Eastern Washington Univeristy, University of Idaho, and Central Washington University.
