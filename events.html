Christmas Toy Drive

Date: Dec. 16th, 2017
Location: MT. Zion Baptist Church

The church was doing a toy drive and needed brothers to assist in crowd management and registration. Bro. Hunde and Bro. Rogers oversaw the managing the amount of people allowed in the building at a given moment. While, Bro. Tillmon oversaw registering patrons as well as consent forms for event photos. We held these positions until the event ended.

Pictured: Bro. Charles Tillmon, Bro. Hanibal Hunde, Bro. Reginald Rogers Jr., Bro. Robert Gary

Thanksgiving Meal Preparation

Date: Nov. 23rd, 2017
Location: Immaculate Conception Church

Brothers came together to assist in the preparation of food to be served to those in the community that needed a helping hand during the holidays. The food was cooked by designated chefs and our responsibilities consisted of getting the ready food into serving trays and creating individual meals. Once those meals were packaged into bags they were transported to vehicles which were brought to the families at a separate location. At this point we initiated clean up and ended the event.

Pictured: Bro. Charles Tillmon, Bro. Hanibal Hunde, Bro. Reginald Rogers Jr., Bro. Nahom Abraha
